Constructive and Opinionated Dialoges for Engagement
(C.O.D.E.)

CODE is an attempt to solve the problems that face India today. When opinionated people come together and engage via dialoges to produce constructive, India develops.

The goal of this site is to trigger such conversations and participate in it to produce India scale solutions.
